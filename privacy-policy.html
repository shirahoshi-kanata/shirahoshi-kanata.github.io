1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Shirahoshi Kanata ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information in connection with our music content and associated tools, including any integration with TikTok's platform via the TikTok API.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with our content or services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Public TikTok account data accessed through the TikTok API (e.g., video statistics, follower counts) for content performance analysis
  • Aggregated analytics data (views, likes, shares, comments) used solely to improve content strategy
  • Contact information you voluntarily provide when reaching out to us

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, financial data, or government identification.

3. How We Use the Information

Information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Analyzing content performance to improve music and video quality
  • Automating content publishing workflows (scheduling, posting) via TikTok's Content Posting API
  • Responding to inquiries from fans and collaborators

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. TikTok API Usage

Our use of the TikTok API complies with TikTok's API Developer Policy and TikTok's Privacy Policy. Data obtained through the TikTok API is used only for the purposes described in this policy and is not stored beyond what is necessary for operational functions.

5. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained only as long as necessary for content strategy purposes. Data obtained via the TikTok API is not stored permanently. You may request deletion of any data associated with you by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

Our workflow may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • TikTok – content publishing and analytics
  • Google Drive – file storage
  • Anthropic Claude – AI-assisted content creation
  • Suno AI – AI music generation

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any data collection at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the address below.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we process. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
